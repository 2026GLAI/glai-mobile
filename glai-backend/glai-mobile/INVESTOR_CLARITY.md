## Purpose

This document defines how GLAi is presented to investors,
partners, and stakeholders without hype, exaggeration, or illusion.

Clarity protects the product.

---

## Core Principle

GLAi is not an AI breakthrough.
It is a focused service built on restraint.

Value comes from design decisions,
not from model size.

---

## What GLAi Is

GLAi is:

- a localized digital interface for Greece
- designed for everyday questions and guidance
- optimized for mobile-first usage
- built to be calm, reliable, and predictable

It prioritizes usefulness over novelty.

---

## What GLAi Is NOT

GLAi is not:

- a general-purpose AI
- a replacement for experts
- a decision-making authority
- a data-harvesting platform
- a disruptive political or legal tool

Any expectation beyond this is incorrect.

---

## Growth Philosophy

GLAi grows by:

- trust
- partnerships
- physical presence (QR codes, points of access)
- cultural integration

Not by aggressive user acquisition
or viral mechanics.

---

## Technology Transparency

GLAi uses existing AI models
chosen for speed, cost-efficiency, and reliability.

The product value lies in:
- behavior design
- language strategy
- system boundaries

Model replacement is possible
without redefining the product.

---

## Risk Awareness

Key risks are acknowledged:

- dependency on external AI providers
- regulatory changes
- misuse attempts

GLAi mitigates risk through:
- architectural isolation
- conservative scope
- trust-first decisions

---

## Monetization Reality

Monetization is expected to be:

- gradual
- partnership-based
- service-aligned

GLAi avoids:
- aggressive paywalls
- deceptive freemium traps
- selling user data

- overpromising

GLAi chooses longevity over valuation.
